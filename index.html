How to submit a report

1 — Scan the location barcode

Every hut, track, toilet, and campsite has one. Scan or enter the location ID.

2 — Take a photo of the issue

Clear, close, and safe. One good photo helps maintenance teams act faster.

3 — Describe the problem

Short and simple is perfect — what, where, and how urgent.

4 — Submit

Your report goes straight to DOC maintenance for triage and action.

Report an Issue

Step 1 — Find the location barcode

Every hut, track, toilet, and campsite has one. If you can't find it, note the nearest landmark.

Step 2 — Take a photo

Take a clear photo from a safe distance. Capture the problem and surrounding context.

Step 3 — Describe the issue

Keep it short: what is broken, where exactly, and any immediate hazards.

Step 4 — Submit

Submit and optionally add contact details if you want follow-up. Otherwise reports are anonymous.

Important Information

Safety First

Never put yourself at risk to take a photo or inspect damage. If it's an emergency, call 111.

Examples of what to report

damaged huts, blocked tracks, damaged signs, overflowing toilets, hazards to people or wildlife.

Examples of what Not to Report

Emergencies, lost persons, or anything requiring immediate rescue — call emergency services.
